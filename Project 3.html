Project 3: Blinkit Dashboard - Power BI

Overview

This project involves the creation of an interactive Power BI dashboard for Blinkit. It provides comprehensive insights into sales, customer behavior, and operational metrics, helping stakeholders optimize business performance.

Key Features

  • Total Sales & Revenue Trends: Track revenue growth over time.
  • Customer Purchase Behavior: Analyze user preferences and buying patterns.
  • Top Selling Items: Identify high-demand products.
  • Outlet Performance Analysis: Evaluate sales by different locations.
  • Ratings & Customer Feedback: Gain insights into customer satisfaction and reviews.
